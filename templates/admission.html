Admission Information

Thank you for your interest in Feni Central High School. Our admission process is designed to be as simple and transparent as possible. We are excited to welcome new students and families to our community.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or proof of age
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Medical certificate (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Download the application form from our website or collect it from the school office.
  2. Fill out the form completely and accurately.
  3. Submit the completed form along with the required documents to the school office.
  4. Attend the admission interview and assessment (if applicable).
  5. Receive the admission decision and complete the enrollment process.

Important Dates

Our admission cycle begins in January and ends in March. Key dates will be posted on our website and school notice board.

Fees and Scholarships

Details about tuition fees and available scholarships can be obtained from the school office. We offer a range of scholarships based on merit and financial need to support our students.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age requirement for admission?

Children must be at least 6 years old to be eligible for admission to the first grade.

Can we visit the school before applying?

Yes, we encourage prospective students and parents to visit the school. Please contact our admissions office to schedule a tour.

Is there an entrance exam?

Yes, there is an entrance exam for certain grades. Details will be provided during the application process.
