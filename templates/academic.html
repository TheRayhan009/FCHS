Academic Overview

Feni Central High School offers a comprehensive curriculum that ensures our students receive a well-rounded education. Our academic programs are designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for learning.

We offer a variety of subjects, including Mathematics, Science, Literature, History, and the Arts, to cater to the diverse interests and strengths of our students.

Our experienced faculty members are dedicated to providing personalized guidance and support to help each student achieve their academic goals.

With state-of-the-art facilities and modern classrooms, Feni Central High School provides an enriching educational experience that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond.

Join us and explore the exciting academic opportunities that await you at Feni Central High School.

Subjects Offered

Mathematics

Our Mathematics program develops logical reasoning and problem-solving skills.

Science

The Science curriculum covers Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, fostering a deep understanding of the natural world.

Literature

Our Literature courses explore classic and contemporary works, enhancing students' analytical and interpretive skills.

History

The History program provides insights into past events and their impact on the present and future.

English

Focuses on grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, and writing skills. This subject aims to develop students' proficiency in the English language, enhancing their ability to communicate effectively both in written and spoken forms.

Bangla

Focuses on grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, and writing skills. This subject aims to develop students' proficiency in the Bangla language, enhancing their ability to communicate effectively both in written and spoken forms.

Islam

Covers the fundamental beliefs and practices of Islam. Students learn about the Five Pillars of Islam, key historical events, and the life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).Focuses on the study of the Quran, including its history, themes, and interpretations. Students also learn about the proper recitation (Tajweed) and memorization (Hifz) of selected verses.

Hindu

Provides an introduction to the fundamental beliefs and practices of Hinduism. Students learn about key concepts such as Dharma, Karma, Moksha, and the various paths to spiritual liberation. Focuses on the classical language of Hindu scriptures. Students learn grammar, vocabulary, and the ability to read and interpret ancient texts such as the Vedas, Upanishads, and epics like the Mahabharata and Ramayana.

ICT

Covers the basic concepts and components of ICT, including hardware, software, networks, and the internet. Students learn about the role and impact of ICT in modern society.Focuses on the structure and function of computer systems and networks. Topics include operating systems, computer architecture, data communication, and network topologies.

Art

Encourages creativity and self-expression through various mediums such as drawing, painting, and sculpture.

Physical Education

Promotes physical fitness and a healthy lifestyle through sports, exercises, and outdoor activities.

Geography

Explores physical and human geography, environmental issues, and the relationship between people and their environments.

Music

Develops musical skills and appreciation through theory, instrumental practice, and vocal training.
